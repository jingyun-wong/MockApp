Portfolio Quality Details The quality of your portfolio is determined on the basis of the number of breached quality criteria, The number of issues within a quality criterion or a group of quality criteria is irrelevant. The stars for the determination of the portfolio quality are assigned as follows: Analysis by asset classes and risk analysis (if available): If the asset allocation as well as the portfolio risk lie within the target bandwidths, the portfolio quality will be evaluated with 3.5 stars. If one of the two criteria is breached, the quality will be evaluated with 1.75 stars. For portfolios where no portfolio risk can be determined, 3.5 stars are assigned for the determination of the asset allocation. Equities and bonds with a sell recommendation as well as equities, bonds and investment funds that are not monitored: If the portfolios contains no equities and bonds with a sell recommendation and no equities, bonds and investment funds that are not monitored, the portfolio quality will be evaluated with a further star. Bulk risks, issuer concentration and bonds with a low rating: if the portfolio has no bulk risks or issuer concentration and no bonds with a low rating are held, the portfolio quality will be evaluated with another half star. Hence, the quality of a portfolio can be evaluated with a maximum of five stars Back
